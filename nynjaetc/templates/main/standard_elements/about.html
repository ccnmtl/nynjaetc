About

CCNMTL and the New York/New Jersey AIDS Education and Training Center created learn.nynjaetc, an online learning effort to educate health professionals about new advances in treating people living with HIV. The online courses are self paced and contain lectures by experts, patient case studies, and interactive activities. The expected audience is doctors and nurses in the New York and New Jersey region, but the courses are be open to anyone who wishes to learn about health care for people living with HIV.
