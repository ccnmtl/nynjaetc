About

CCNMTL and the New York/New Jersey AIDS Education and Training Center created learn.nynjaetc, an online learning effort to educate healthcare professionals about new advances in treating people living with HIV. The online courses are self-paced and contain lectures by experts, patient case studies, and interactive activities. The expected audience is doctors and nurses in the New York and New Jersey region, but the courses are open to anyone who wishes to learn about healthcare for people living with HIV/AIDS.
