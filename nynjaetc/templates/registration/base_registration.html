Online Training and Education For Healthcare Professionals Providing Care and Services For People Living With HIV

learn.nynjaetc is a series of online learning modules and activities created by the NY/NJ AIDS Education and Training Center (NY/NJ AETC) and designed to help healthcare professionals learn more about caring for people living with HIV. The first course available at learn.nynjaetc.org is Hepatitis C Medications and Special Considerations for People Living with HIV.


The NY/NJ AETC provides education, training and consultation services to health care professionals in New York and New Jersey to improve access to and quality of HIV/AIDS care.


For more information on clinical training opportunities please contact:

Columbia University

 or visit our website www.nynjaetc.org.

The NY/NJ AETC is supported by grant number H4AHA00071 from the Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) AIDS Education and Training Centers Program.
